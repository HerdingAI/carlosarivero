How to make AI products

Over the past few years I've been organizing my learning and experience — shipping machine-learning, generative, and agentic systems in regulated industries — into a course. It is free, it asks nothing of you, and it exists to give back to the community. The CORE sequence (01–13) is a complete operating model on its own, capped by a capstone checkpoint; DEPTH units (14–24) go deeper on demand.
