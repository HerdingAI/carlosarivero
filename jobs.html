Jobs

Job hunting as a product manager means checking a dozen boards, each with its own noise. Product Careers is my answer: a system that aggregates product-management roles from across the industry into one curated board — deduplicated, categorized, and kept current.

It's free, there's no catch, and it exists because the product community has been generous with me and this is one way to return the favor.

If you're a PM looking for your next role —
Browse the board at product-careers.com →
How it works
1The system continuously aggregates PM openings from company career pages and the major boards.
2Listings are deduplicated and categorized — by seniority, domain, and remote/on-site — so you filter once instead of searching twelve times.
3Stale postings age out automatically. What you see is current, or close to it.
